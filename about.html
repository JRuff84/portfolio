Artist / Graphic Designer

Working in both physical mediums as well as digital, I love to create. Originally focusing on sketch work through mediums like graphite and ink on paper, and then later discovering charcoal before venturing into digital mediums like photoshop and graphics tablets, I've created branding and marketing material for artists, musician, and performers in addition to small businesses and companies. I've been honored to collaborate with Sean Moll (DC Comics) and have had had work featured in films presented in both Sundance and Cannes film festivals.
